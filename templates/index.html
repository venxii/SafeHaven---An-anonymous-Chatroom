BELIEVING

Focusing On What Matters Most

One of the primary challenges witnesses face is the harm after disclosing information about a crime.

Additionally, witnesses may face intimidation, coercion, or pressure to remain silent, further complicating their decision to come forward. They may also encounter practical obstacles such as navigating the legal system, understanding their rights, or accessing resources for support and protection.

Here, we focus on these witnesses who need guidance to figure out their next move and having an application where they can safely communicate with lawyers without disclosing their identity and without letting the perpetrators know their next move.
